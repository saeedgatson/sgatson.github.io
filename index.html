How it all started.

I'm A Geek With Jock Tendencies

I was finishing up my Computer Science Degree at Cal State East Bay back in 2011. Trading my time between writing code, and grabbing rebounds for the colleges Division II Basketball team.

So I'm watching some random action movie with a beautiful girl (now wife), and started noticing something was off in almost every scene.

The car door in the background was open, next cut it’s closed. Bad guys magically falling down without being touched. My wife, wasn’t catching any of this. She was amazed the first 2 times, a little freaked out at 4, and annoyed by 6.

So I stopped talking around then. Quietly found a ton of “bugs” that day. Didn’t know it at the time but a testers seed was starting to grow.

For over 4 years that tree has grown, and I've helping startups success with test automation ever senses.
